Houston, TX

Houston Neighborhood Guide

The best places to live for travel nurses — organized by hospital, with commute times, rent ranges, and what each area is really like.

🗺️
How to use this guide: Find your assigned hospital below. Each section lists the top neighborhoods for nurses at that facility, with commute estimates, rent ranges, and local highlights. All rent ranges are for furnished short-term rentals (1–3 months) as of early 2026.
Texas Medical Center

Texas Medical Center / NRG Area

The TMC is the world's largest medical complex, spanning over 50 institutions including Houston Methodist, Texas Children's, Memorial Hermann, MD Anderson, and more. These neighborhoods put you within a short commute — or even walking distance.

Medical Center / NRG Park

🏥 Texas Medical Center — 0–5 min commute
💵 $1,800–$3,200/mo
🚗 <5 min
🐾 Some options
  • Closest possible option to TMC campus
  • Many furnished corporate apartments available
  • Adjacent to Hermann Park and the Zoo
  • Easy access to the Light Rail (METRORail)
  • Quieter, more residential feel

Midtown

🏥 Texas Medical Center — 10–15 min
💵 $1,500–$2,800/mo
🚗 10–15 min
🐾 Good options
  • Vibrant bar and restaurant scene
  • Walkable to groceries and coffee shops
  • METRORail Green Line access
  • Great for nurses who want to socialize
  • Wide range of furnished apartment options

Montrose

🏥 Texas Medical Center — 10–18 min
💵 $1,600–$3,000/mo
🚗 10–18 min
🐾 Very pet-friendly
  • Eclectic, artsy, and walkable
  • Outstanding food and coffee scene
  • Very pet-welcoming landlords
  • Shorter commute than it looks on a map
  • Close to the Museum District

Museum District

🏥 Texas Medical Center — 8–12 min
💵 $1,700–$3,200/mo
🚗 8–12 min
🐾 Moderate options
  • Quiet, tree-lined streets
  • Near Hermann Park — great for walking/running
  • METRORail access nearby
  • Close to Rice University and Greenway Plaza
  • Upscale, safe neighborhood feel

West University Place

🏥 Texas Medical Center — 12–18 min
💵 $2,000–$4,000/mo
🚗 12–18 min
🐾 Some options
  • One of Houston's safest neighborhoods
  • Excellent schools (great for nurses with kids)
  • Quiet suburban feel close to TMC
  • Higher price point but premium quality
  • Strong sense of community

Greenway Plaza / Upper Kirby

🏥 Texas Medical Center — 12–20 min
💵 $1,500–$2,800/mo
🚗 12–20 min
🐾 Good options
  • Mix of upscale apartments and condos
  • Very walkable stretch of Westheimer
  • Close to Whole Foods, Trader Joe's, and Target
  • Great restaurants and bars nearby
  • Corporate furnished apartments abundant
North Houston

The Woodlands Area

The Woodlands hosts Memorial Hermann The Woodlands Medical Center, HCA Houston Healthcare The Woodlands, and Houston Methodist The Woodlands. A master-planned community with excellent quality of life.

The Woodlands Town Center

🏥 Memorial Hermann Woodlands — 5–12 min
💵 $1,800–$3,500/mo
🚗 5–12 min
🐾 Some options
  • Walkable Waterway area with shops and restaurants
  • Beautiful green spaces and trails
  • Very safe, master-planned community
  • Furnished apartments and corporate housing available

Shenandoah / Spring

🏥 The Woodlands hospitals — 10–20 min
💵 $1,300–$2,400/mo
🚗 10–20 min
🐾 Good options
  • More affordable alternative to Woodlands proper
  • Easy highway access via I-45
  • Good selection of apartment communities
  • Budget-friendly furnished rentals available
West Houston

Energy Corridor / Katy Area

West Houston hosts Memorial Hermann Katy, Houston Methodist West, HCA Houston Healthcare West, and St. Luke's Health — Baylor St. Luke's Medical Center West Campus. Great option for nurses who prefer suburban comfort.

Energy Corridor

🏥 Memorial Hermann / Methodist West — 10–20 min
💵 $1,400–$2,600/mo
🚗 10–20 min
🐾 Good options
  • Corporate apartments plentiful (major energy companies nearby)
  • Terry Hershey Park trail along Buffalo Bayou
  • Multiple grocery options and dining
  • Good highway access (I-10 westbound)

Katy

🏥 Memorial Hermann Katy — 5–15 min
💵 $1,300–$2,500/mo
🚗 5–15 min
🐾 Very pet-friendly
  • Very affordable for furnished short-term rentals
  • Extremely pet-friendly community
  • LaCenterra at Cinco Ranch — great dining and shopping
  • Quiet suburban lifestyle, excellent safety
South Houston

Pearland & Southeast Houston

South Houston hosts HCA Houston Healthcare Pearland, UTMB Health, and serves nurses traveling from the Galveston/Clear Lake corridor. Also within range of TMC via the 288 corridor.

Pearland

🏥 HCA Pearland / TMC via 288 — 15–30 min
💵 $1,300–$2,400/mo
🚗 15–30 min
🐾 Very pet-friendly
  • One of Houston's fastest-growing suburbs
  • Great value for space compared to inner loop
  • Lots of newer apartment communities with amenities
  • 288 expressway gives quick TMC access (off-peak)

Clear Lake / Webster

🏥 UTMB / HCA SE Houston — 10–20 min
💵 $1,200–$2,200/mo
🚗 10–20 min
🐾 Good options
  • Near NASA Johnson Space Center
  • Beautiful waterfront areas on Clear Lake
  • Affordable furnished options
  • Spacious, suburban, family-friendly feel
Inner Loop & Northwest

The Heights & Inner Loop

Great for nurses at Ben Taub General Hospital (Harris Health), Houston Methodist Hospital, or anyone who wants a walkable, characterful neighborhood while staying close to the city center.

The Heights

🏥 Ben Taub / Downtown hospitals — 10–20 min
💵 $1,600–$3,200/mo
🚗 10–20 min
🐾 Very pet-friendly
  • Charming historic bungalows and modern apartments
  • Heights Hike & Bike Trail for fitness
  • Incredible brunch and coffee scene on 19th St
  • Extremely pet-welcoming
  • Community farmers market on weekends

EaDo (East Downtown)

🏥 Ben Taub / Memorial Hermann Downtown — 5–12 min
💵 $1,400–$2,600/mo
🚗 5–12 min
🐾 Moderate options
  • Up-and-coming area with great restaurants and bars
  • Close to Minute Maid Park (Astros games!)
  • Newer apartment buildings with modern amenities
  • METRORail Green Line access

Oak Forest / Garden Oaks

🏥 Northwest Houston hospitals — 10–20 min
💵 $1,400–$2,600/mo
🚗 10–20 min
🐾 Good options
  • Hidden gem with a strong local identity
  • Great local dining and breweries
  • Good value for furnished rentals
  • Quieter than Midtown but still lively
