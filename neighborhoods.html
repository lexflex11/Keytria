Houston guide

Know before you arrive

Every major neighborhood near Houston's hospitals — commute, cost, and vibe.

TMC / MD Anderson

Medical Center

The world's largest medical complex. Walking distance to TMC, MD Anderson, and Memorial Hermann. Premium location, premium prices.

Trendy

Midtown

Walkable, vibrant, and affordable. Great restaurant and nightlife scene. Easy commute to TMC via light rail.

Quiet & safe

Museum District

Tree-lined streets, world-class museums, and a calm atmosphere. Close to TMC without the Medical Center price tag.

Popular

Montrose

Houston's most eclectic neighborhood. Art galleries, coffee shops, and an active community. Popular with first-time Houston travelers.

Active lifestyle

Rice Military

Along the Buffalo Bayou trails. Lots of young professionals, great bars, and easy access to the Energy Corridor and TMC.

Methodist nurses

Sugar Land

Quiet suburban living southwest of downtown. Ideal for nurses at Houston Methodist Sugar Land. Great schools and safe streets.

North Houston

The Woodlands

Master-planned community 30 miles north of Houston. Resort-style living with excellent amenities. Close to Memorial Hermann The Woodlands.

Budget friendly

Pearland

Affordable suburb south of Houston with great value per square foot. Close to HCA Houston Healthcare Pearland and other south Houston facilities.
