The Process

How It Works

From intake form to apartment keys — here's exactly what happens when you work with Keytria.

1
Takes ~5 minutes

Tell us about your assignment

Fill out our detailed intake form with everything we need to find your ideal match: your assigned hospital, move-in date, contract length, budget, number of bedrooms, whether you have pets, and any must-have amenities. The more detail, the better your matches.

  • Include your exact hospital campus if you know it — "Texas Medical Center" vs. "Memorial Hermann in The Woodlands" makes a big difference in our search.
  • Let us know your parking situation — many Houston apartments have paid parking and we factor that into your total budget.
  • If your contract might extend, tell us — we'll prioritize properties with flexible lease extensions.
Start Your Intake Form →
2
Within 48 hours

We curate your personalized shortlist

Your dedicated concierge reviews your intake form and searches our vetted property network — plus external listings we've pre-screened — to build a shortlist of 3 to 5 options that genuinely match your criteria. We don't just dump search results on you.

  • Every listing includes a verified commute time to your hospital (driving and public transit where applicable).
  • We contact landlords on your behalf to confirm availability before presenting an option to you.
  • We include a pros/cons summary for each option so you can compare at a glance.
  • VIP Concierge clients receive virtual tour scheduling in addition to photos and floor plans.
3
You're in control

Review, ask questions, and choose

We send your curated matches by email with a detailed breakdown of each property. You can reply with questions and we'll get answers fast. When you're ready, let us know which one you want to move forward with.

  • Not feeling any of the options? We'll search again at no extra charge until you find the right fit.
  • Can't decide between two? We can help you weigh trade-offs — commute vs. amenities, cost vs. space, etc.
  • Full Relocation and VIP clients get a phone consultation to walk through options together.
4
Keys in hand

We handle lease coordination & move-in prep

Once you choose your apartment, we step in to coordinate the lease signing, review terms on your behalf (Full Relocation & VIP), and make sure everything is set for your arrival. We hand you a Houston move-in checklist so day one goes smoothly.

  • We review your lease for common red flags: auto-renewal traps, early termination fees, and hidden pet fees.
  • We provide a utility setup guide specific to Houston (CenterPoint Energy, Houston Water, etc.).
  • VIP clients get a 30-day post-move-in check-in to make sure everything is going well.
  • We're reachable by email (and phone for VIP) if any issues come up after you arrive.
