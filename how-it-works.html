How it works

From intake form to keys in hand

We do the heavy lifting so you can focus on your assignment.

1

Submit your intake form

Tell us your hospital assignment, move-in date, budget range, and must-haves. The form takes about 5 minutes and gives us everything we need to start your search.

2

We research everything

Your concierge searches vetted apartment databases, contacts properties directly, and verifies commute times to your specific hospital. We only surface options that actually work for you.

3

You get a personalized shortlist

Within 48 hours you'll receive a side-by-side comparison of the top options — with photos, floor plans, commute estimates, amenities, and lease details.

4

We help you close

Once you've chosen your apartment, we coordinate tours, support your application, review the lease, and set up utilities. On Full Relocation and VIP packages, we're with you through move-in day.
