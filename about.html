Our Story

We Built This Because We Felt the Problem

Keytria started with a simple frustration: travel nurses arriving in Houston — one of the most complex cities in the country to navigate — with only a few days to find an apartment before their assignment started. We're here to fix that.

Our Mission

Making Houston feel like home, before you arrive

Travel nurses do some of the most important and demanding work in healthcare. The last thing you should have to worry about during a new assignment is where you're going to sleep — or whether your lease has a predatory auto-renewal clause buried in the fine print.

Keytria specializes exclusively in Houston. We know every neighborhood, every major hospital's parking situation, which landlords are responsive and which aren't, and where to find pet-friendly furnished apartments that don't require a 12-month lease. That local depth is what makes the difference.

We're not a listing aggregator. We're not a property manager. We're an independent apartment-matching service that works on your behalf — and we're paid directly by you so our incentives are always aligned with finding you the best option, not the most profitable one for us.
