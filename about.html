About Keytria

Houston's apartment concierge built specifically for travel nurses and relocating professionals.

Travel nursing is one of the most rewarding careers in healthcare — but finding a safe, furnished, short term apartment in an unfamiliar city on a tight timeline is one of the hardest parts of the job. Most nurses spend 10–15 hours scrolling through listings only to feel overwhelmed and uncertain.

Keytria was built to fix that. We're an independent apartment matching concierge for travel nurses coming to Houston. We do the research, build the shortlist, and guide you through the process — so you can show up focused on your assignment, not your housing situation.

We work on your behalf — not on behalf of landlords or property managers. Every match we send you has been manually vetted for commute distance, lease flexibility, and furnished availability. We've built relationships with properties across Houston's major medical corridors, and we know which ones actually deliver on their listings.
